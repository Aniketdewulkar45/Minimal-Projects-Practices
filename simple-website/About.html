The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) logo is a circular emblem featuring a roaring lion at its center, 
  set against a background of bright yellow, with the team's name written in a bold font.
  The logo represents the team's power and connects to their nickname, "Lions," 
  as well as their home ground, Chepauk, known as the "lion's den".
