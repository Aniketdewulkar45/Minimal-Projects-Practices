MIND BLOWING SHIBUYA CROSSING

On my trip to japan i discovered What makes Shibuya Crossing unique? Unlike typical intersections where pedestrians cross one direction at a time, Shibuya Crossing is known for its scramble style. All traffic stops, allowing people to cross diagonally and from all sides at once in a visually chaotic, yet organized, flow of people.

"Travel expands the mind"
Shibuya is famous for the iconic and busy Shibuya Scramble Crossing, its status as Tokyo's youth and fashion hub, vibrant shopping and dining, trend-setting culture, and lively nightlife.
